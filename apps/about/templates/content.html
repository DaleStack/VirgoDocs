About Virgo

Virgo is a Python web framework built by a developer who believes web development should be simple, fast, and genuinely enjoyable.

My Story

Virgo was born from a common challenge: the struggle to learn and master complex web frameworks. As a developer who experienced this firsthand, I set out to create a Python web framework that prioritizes simplicity and ease of learning, making web development accessible and enjoyable for everyone, especially beginners.

Core Features

  • Simple: Easy to understand and use — designed to help you focus on building, not battling complexity.
  • Fast: Runs quickly and stays out of your way.
  • Approachable: Built for beginners — no experience needed. Virgo makes learning web development feel natural.
  • Flexible: Start small, customize freely, and build your way. Virgo adapts to your ideas, not the other way around.
